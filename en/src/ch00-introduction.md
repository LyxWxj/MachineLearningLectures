# Computational Neuroscience & Machine Learning

## Course Overview

This course is based on **Neuromatch Academy's** Computational Neuroscience curriculum, providing a systematic journey from mathematical foundations through core machine learning algorithms to cutting-edge topics in computational neuroscience.

## Learning Path

The course is divided into three parts. Follow this recommended order:

### Part I: Foundation Lectures

| Order | Topic | Content |
|-------|-------|---------|
| 0 | **Math Foundations** | Calculus, Linear Algebra, Probability — the three mathematical pillars of ML |
| 1 | **ML Fundamentals** | What is ML, learning paradigms, workflow & terminology |
| 2 | **Metrics, Loss & Optimization** | Evaluation metrics, loss functions, gradient descent & optimizers |
| 3 | **The Bayes Family** | Bayes' theorem, prior & posterior, Naive Bayes, Bayesian networks |

### Part II: Neuromatch Lab Notebooks

| Week | Topic | Content |
|------|-------|---------|
| Week 0 | **Python & Math Foundations** | LIF neuron model, spikes & refractory periods, linear algebra & calculus hands-on |
| Week 1 | **Model Fitting & ML** | Regression, classification, model selection, cross-validation |
| Week 2 | **Linear Systems & Deep Learning** | Linear dynamical systems, neural network basics, backpropagation |
| Week 3 | **Bayesian Brain & RL** | Bayesian inference, Markov decision processes, reinforcement learning basics |

### Part III: Advanced Topics

| Topic | Content |
|-------|---------|
| **Markov Processes & Kalman Filtering** | Hidden Markov Models (HMM), Kalman filter, forward inference & smoothing |

## Prerequisites

- **Programming**: Python basics (NumPy, Matplotlib)
- **Math**: High school math foundations (Lecture 0 reviews all required math)

## Recommended Study Approach

1. Complete the **Foundation Lectures** (Lectures 0-2 + Bayes Family) to build the theoretical framework
2. Work through the **Neuromatch Lab Notebooks** week by week for hands-on practice
3. Study the **Advanced Topics** to broaden your understanding
